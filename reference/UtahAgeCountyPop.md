# Population size by age group for counties in Utah

United States Census Bureau resident population estimates by selected
age groups for each county in the state of Utah in 2024

## Usage

``` r
UtahAgeCountyPop
```

## Format

A data frame with 29 rows for the 29 counties of Utah and 23 columns:

- county:

  County name

- total:

  Total population estimate

- age0to4:

  Age 0 to 4 years population estimate

- age5to9:

  Age 5 to 9 years population estimate

- age10to13:

  Age 10 to 13 years population estimate

- age14:

  Age 14 years population estimate

- age15:

  Age 15 years population estimate

- age16to17:

  Age 16 to 17 years population estimate

- age18to19:

  Age 18 to 19 years population estimate

- age20to24:

  age 20 to 24 years population estimate

- age25to29:

  age 25 to 29 years population estimate

- age30to34:

  age 30 to 34 years population estimate

- age35to39:

  age 35 to 39 years population estimate

- age40to44:

  age 40 to 44 years population estimate

- age45to49:

  age 45 to 49 years population estimate

- age50to54:

  age 50 to 54 years population estimate

- age55to59:

  age 55 to 59 years population estimate

- age60to64:

  age 60 to 64 years population estimate

- age65to69:

  age 65 to 69 years population estimate

- age70to74:

  age 70 to 74 years population estimate

- age75to79:

  age 75 to 79 years population estimate

- age80to84:

  age 80 to 84 years population estimate

- age85plus:

  age 85 years and older population estimate
